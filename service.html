OUR BESPOKE SERVICE

your journey with sastré starts here...

Sastré blends the English craftsmanship precision with the French tailor’s attention to detail.

Drawing its inspiration from your unique contemporary daily life, Sastré provides its quality expertise and custom designs to reinvent your couture statement and enhance your wardrobe, We aim to reveal your true self and provide you with your perfect fit.

The Sastré experience starts with a visit to the Shoreditch Studio*. We’re always open for those curious to begin their journey towards their ideal fit & tailoring.
To reach the desired fit we require a minimum of two fitting checks on your garments.
 *Our clients are based all around Europe, so we’re used to a bit of distance… We’ll tend to your needs at your preferred location, whether it’s in Paris, London, Luxembourg or Madrid!
At Sastré, we aim to develop a bond with our clients.

As we start understanding your silhouette along with your desires, we will provide you with a uniquely tailored garment, catered to your needs. Throughout your journey, you will have the opportunity to view and discover your desired fabric amongst an elegant selection sourced from renowned English and Italian mills, including Holland & Sherry, Harrisons, Dormeuil, Ermenegildo Zegna and Loro Piana
The Sastré journey continues with a bespoke styling service aimed at defining your very own quintessential look. At Sastré, we focus on the most intricate details and accompany you through every step of your fitting experience.
